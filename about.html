Who I am and what I do

Who I am

I was born in the UK in 1975, raised in London and Paris, studied in Manchester, lived & worked for 15 years in Berlin and now live in Biel with my wife & 2 boys. I am constantly looking for new challenges. Upon concluding my degree in Modern Languages, I moved to Berlin, completed MBA Diplomas in Economics, Marketing and Management and travelled the world learning by doing in a variety of professional capacities ranging from project management, marketing and market research to engineering, reconstruction, proposals & contracts & food. I have always been fascinated with the user experience. Having recently completed an all-encompaasing UX design course from the Career Foundry in Berlin, the improvement of users' interaction with mobile and desktop products has become my new goal!
